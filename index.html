    What is Cyber Security

    Cybersecurity is the practice of safeguarding systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. These threats often aim to compromise sensitive information, disrupt services, or steal resources. Through proactive strategies and protective measures, cybersecurity ensures the safety of digital operations, helping businesses maintain secure environments and prevent breaches..

    In addition to protection, cybersecurity also focuses on detecting and responding to incidents, recovering from attacks, and minimizing damage. It encompasses various domains, including network security, information security, operational security, and disaster recovery, each playing a critical role in defending against persistent cyber threats.
